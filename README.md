# Retail Banking Customer Insights Platform

## Overview

The Retail Banking Customer Insights Platform is an end-to-end banking analytics solution designed to analyze customer behavior, product adoption, digital engagement, and customer value across retail banking segments.

This project leverages SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI to transform customer data into actionable business insights, enabling financial institutions to improve customer engagement, optimize cross-selling strategies, and drive revenue growth through data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Retail banks serve diverse customer segments with varying financial needs, product preferences, and engagement patterns. Understanding customer behavior is critical for increasing customer retention, improving product adoption, and maximizing customer lifetime value.

The objective of this project is to:

- Analyze customer demographics and banking behavior
- Evaluate product adoption across customer segments
- Monitor digital banking engagement
- Identify high-value customer groups
- Discover cross-selling opportunities
- Support customer-centric business strategies

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze retail banking customer profiles
- Segment customers based on value and engagement
- Evaluate product penetration and adoption trends
- Assess digital banking usage patterns
- Identify revenue-generating customer segments
- Build executive dashboards for customer intelligence

## Dataset Overview

The dataset consists of simulated retail banking customer information including:

- Customer Demographics
- Account Information
- Deposit Balances
- Loan Relationships
- Product Holdings
- Digital Banking Usage
- Customer Segments
- Income Categories
- Geographic Information
- Customer Engagement Metrics

### Dataset Scale

- 100,000+ Retail Banking Customers
- ₹500M+ Deposits Analyzed
- ₹300M+ Lending Exposure Evaluated
- Multiple Banking Products
- Multi-Region Customer Coverage
